Hello :wave: I'm David Graff, a joint Ph.D. student in the [Coley group](https://coley.mit.edu) at MIT ChemE and [Shakhnovich group](https://faculty.chemistry.harvard.edu/shakhnovich) at Harvard CCB. In my studies, I focus on developing algorithms and software to accelerate computational drug discovery at ultra-large scales. I'm broadly interested in merging novel techniques in optimization with the unique contexts of massive computing problems and thinking about new ways in which computers can aid in the identification of new chemistries.
